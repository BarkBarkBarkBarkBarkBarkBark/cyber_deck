Three reference builds:

1. **Hosaka Desktop** (`desktop`) — tabletop, detachable keyboard, Orange Pi 4 Pro
2. **Hosaka Portable** (`portable`) — clamshell, agent-ready, Orange Pi 4 Pro
3. **Hosaka Wearable** (`wearable`) — wrist-mounted, Orange Pi Zero 3W

to every build automatically by the parts matrix.
